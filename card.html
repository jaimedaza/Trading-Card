Bengal Tiger

Unlike most members of the cat family, tigers like water. They are good swimmers and often cool off in pools or streams.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Average Length: 270 to 310 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 8 to 10 years
  • Habitat: Asia (India, Bangladesh, Nepal)

Tigers live alone and aggressively scent-mark large territories to keep their rivals away. They are powerful nocturnal hunters that travel many miles to find buffalo, deer, wild pigs, and other large mammals.
